Finding Nebro

  • Psycholigcal research & environmental research
  • Comparing different profiles, search for a perfect match
  • First meet-up of the fishes
  • Evaluation & reaction

Fix Your Fish is known for project Finding Nebro. We strive to find your fish a friend for life that matches your fish in all areas. By means of extensive research we’ll manage to find a perfect friend for your pet.

We perform a psychological research of your fish where we create a profile that indicates your fish’s personality. This shows the different character traits, temperament, hobbies, social attitude and more. Taking into account the living environment of your fish, our psychologists compare different profiles in search for the perfect match.

After the perfect match is sorted out, there will be a first encounter. This meeting is thoroughly examined. We analyse the attitudes of both fish. If your fish is not completely satisfied, we look for another friend until both parties are happy. That way your fish has a friend for life!
